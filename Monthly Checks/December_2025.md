# Monthly Health & Performance Review

This repository documents a **monthly, data-driven review of health and training performance** using nutrition, exercise, body composition, and heart-rate data.

Every 30 days, a **slide deck is published** summarizing:
- Diet execution (calories, macros, protein consistency)
- Training frequency, volume, and structure
- Heart-rateâ€“based workload and efficiency metrics (e.g., Session Load Index, training density)
- Body composition trends (weight, waist, fat mass, lean mass)
- Clear, quantified action items for the next cycle

The focus is on **execution quality, efficiency, and repeatable decision-making**, not motivation or anecdotes.

## What this is
- A personal performance operating review
- A living analytics project
- A reproducible framework that can be forked or adapted

## What this is not
- A workout program
- A meal plan
- A transformation challenge

## Update cadence
- **Monthly**
- One slide deck per 30-day cycle
- Metrics, insights, and goals reset every review
